How I work and build things

As a self-taught software developer, there are two different types of work for me. On one hand, I am part of a team in the company’s office I am working for, where I utilize my knowledge of Android to implement ideas from scratch. Team-working is the main component here, it’s not much about my idea, it’s more about how to incorporate others’ idea with some of my suggestions along the line.

On the other hand, I am working myself to expose myself to different parts of Computer Science and IT, including iOS app development, Web Development, Machine Learning, and algorithmic studies. I would learn these on my own after work from office, mainly to broaden my capacities to improve my career prospects.

I like to challenge myself in order to explore my limits, setting tasks deadlines that are shorter than what people would normally set; this motivates me to move into a realm of workaholism, which often help me to succeed in many things, because once I kickstarted, I won’t stop, and it allows me to see results after a relatively short period of time.
